Daycare & Nightcare: Where Little Stars Thrive

At Nova Baraka Junior School, we offer a warm, nurturing, and imaginative haven for your little ones aged 2-3, as well as PP1, PP2, and Grades 1-3. Our dedicated caregivers and educators craft a safe, fun-filled environment that sparks creativity, curiosity, and a lifelong love for learning—day or night!

Why Trust Us with Your Child’s Care?

  • Tailored Daycare Fun: Playful, age-appropriate activities for 2-3-year-olds to explore and grow.
  • Peaceful Nightcare Haven: Safe, restful overnight care with soothing routines for preschoolers and early graders.
  • Expert Educators: Loving, trained staff dedicated to PP1, PP2, and Grades 1-3 development.
  • Secure & Joyful Campus: A vibrant, child-friendly space designed for all-day safety and happiness.

Our Programs

PP1 & PP2 (Pre-Primary)

Our pre-primary program introduces young learners to foundational skills through play-based activities, songs, and stories. Focus areas include early literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

Grade 1 to Grade 3

Our primary program builds on foundational skills with a curriculum that includes Math, English, Science, and Creative Arts. We emphasize critical thinking and hands-on learning.

Extracurricular Activities

  • Music and Dance
  • Sports and Outdoor Play
  • Art and Craft Workshops
