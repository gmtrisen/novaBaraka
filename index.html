Our Programs

PP1 & PP2 (Pre-Primary)

Our pre-primary program introduces young learners to foundational skills through play-based activities, songs, and stories. Focus areas include early literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

Grade 1 to Grade 3

Our primary program builds on foundational skills with a curriculum that includes Math, English, Science, and Creative Arts. We emphasize critical thinking and hands-on learning.

Extracurricular Activities

  • Music and Dance
  • Sports and Outdoor Play
  • Art and Craft Workshops
