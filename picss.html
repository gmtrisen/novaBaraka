IN SEARCH OF EXCELLENCE

Thika Road Academy fosters learning in academic, discipline and extracurricular activities. Since the school's founding in 1991, our goal is to offer our students a firm all round foundation and prepare them for success beyond primary school.

In line with the school motto, "In Search of Excellence", the school aims to produce hard working and responsible students who strive to excel in all areas of their development.
